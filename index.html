The Ohio River Valley

In the Ohio River Valley, Cheif Pontiac realized the defeat of his Spanish allies meant his ancestral lands would soon be invaded by English settlers.

Resolving to fight the intruders while he still might win, Pontiac gathered the Ohio River tribes under his leadership and attacked the British forts to the West.
